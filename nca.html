LIGHTNING SQUARE CHESS CLUB

2023 Northern California High School Chess Championship

Lightning Square Chess Club invites all High School Chess Clubs in Northern California and eligible players to compete in the 2023 Northern California High School Chess Championship. The Championship will take place in Willow Glen Branch Library (1157 Minnesota Ave, San Jose, CA 95125) on November 18 2023.

The Championship welcomes participation from every high school located in Northern California. Each high school has the opportunity to field a team consisting of 3 to 4 players, including one alternate. It is a requirement that each player on the team is currently enrolled as a student at the school they are representing. The prize is $300 for the first place team; and $200 for the second place team. Each round, three players from each team play and the boards must be ordered by decreasing USCF rating.

The Championship will be 3-round Swiss tournaments with a time control of G60 + 30. The standard US Chess rules apply. The forfeit time is 30 minutes.

All teams and players have to register before the deadline of November 11, 2023. Please bring your own chess set and clock.
