LIGHTNING SQUARE CHESS CLUB

Welcome

Lightning Square Chess is a youth-led nonprofit organization founded in 2022 by three Northern California chess players: WCM Kally Wen, Wentao (Sean) Wu, and Vincent Qin. Our mission is to bridge geographic and economic disparities in chess education and create opportunities for children to learn and grow through chess.

Chess Club Bootstrap Initiative

This initiative aims to bridge geographic and economic disparities in chess education by partnering with schools, particularly in Africa, to help them establish their own chess clubs. Lightning Square provides online classes and donates chess sets to support these clubs.

Online Chess Classes for Underserved States

We offer free introductory courses specifically designed for residents of U.S. states where chess communities are smaller and educational resources are limited. By focusing on these underserved regions, we aim to provide equitable access to high-quality chess instruction, spark interest in the game, and support the development of new chess communities where opportunities have traditionally been scarce. Through these accessible online programs, we hope to inspire a new generation of players and ensure that geography or financial limitations are never barriers to learning chess.

Bay Area In-person Classes

We offer engaging chess classes and workshops designed to introduce participants to the game and develop their skills in the Bay Area. These classes focus on basics, rules, strategies, problem-solving, and practical application through friendly matches.

Bay Area Chess Tournaments

We regularly host USCF-rated quads tournaments for elementary and middle school students in the Bay Area. These tournaments provide a platform for young players to showcase their skills, compete, and connect with peers.
