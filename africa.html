LIGHTNING SQUARE CHESS CLUB

International Program

Our international program is designed to support schools and communities in starting chess clubs by providing free resources and guidance. We offer online chess classes, complimentary chess sets, and step-by-step support to help new clubs get established. Since its launch, we have partnered with the following schools in Kenya, Nigeria, and South Africa to create opportunities for hundreds of children to learn and enjoy the game of chess.

Our Mission: Bridge geographic and economic disparities in chess education and create opportunities for children to learn and grow through chess.
