Join Us

Join us at lightning square Chess Club and be a part of an engaging community that celebrates the beauty of chess, fosters meaningful connections, and propels the ancient game into the modern spotlight. Together, let's make every move count – on the board and in life.

Our Mission: Bridge geographic and economic disparities in chess education and create opportunities for children to learn and grow through chess.
