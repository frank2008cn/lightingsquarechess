LIGHTNING SQUARE CHESS CLUB

Our Classes

Welcome to our exciting chess course!

This course is designed to introduce participants to the fascinating world of chess and develop their skills in a fun and engaging environment.

Chess is not just a game: It's a mental sport that challenges and stimulates the mind. Through this course, you will develop critical thinking, problem- solving abilities, concentration, and creativity. Chess has been proven to enhance cognitive development and academic performance, making it a valuable skill to acquire.

Course syllabus: Chess basics, rules of chess, piece movements, board setup, objective of the game, capturing, special moves, basic strategies, game notation, problem-solving exercises, and practice games.
