LIGHTNING SQUARE CHESS CLUB

We are proud to be hosting an exciting chess course!

This course is designed to introduce participants to the fascinating world of chess and develop their skills in a fun and engaging environment.

Chess is not just a game; it's a mental sport that challenges and stimulates the mind. Through this course, you will develop critical thinking, problem-solving skills, concentration, and creativity. Chess has been proven to enhance cognitive development and academic performance, making it a valuable skill to acquire.

Course outlines: Chess basics, rules of chess, piece movements, board setup, objective of the game, capturing, special moves, basic strategies, game notation, problem-solving exercises, and practice games.
